I'm excited to unveil a powerful new feature in "Escape The Dungeon Maze" - the Tinting and Texture System!

## Dynamic Item Customization

This new system allows creating multiple different game items with unique colors and textures while using only one 3D model and one icon. Game items like potions, keys, apples, and bread can now be configured with different visual styles directly in the Level Editor.

The benefits are enormous:
- **Efficiency**: One 3D model can create countless variations
- **Flexibility**: Level designers can customize items to match their dungeon themes
- **Variety**: Players encounter more diverse and visually interesting items
