The February 2026 update <span style="background-color: #4CAF50; color: white; padding: 3px 8px; border-radius: 12px; font-size: 0.85em; margin-left: 5px;">R4.2 - February 2026</span> for "The Dungeon - Get Out" and the corresponding Level Editor is now **officially released**!

This update brings several new features and improvements across both the game and the editor.

## Game: What's New

### New Tutorial Campaign

  <img src="/assets/2026/02/Tutorial1.png" alt="Tutorial Campaign" style="width: 48%;">
  <img src="/assets/2026/02/Tutorial2.png" alt="Tutorial Gameplay" style="width: 48%;">

I've been listening to your feedback! A brand new interactive tutorial is now available from the main menu. It walks new players step-by-step through movement, heroes, combat, the map, and menus - so you can feel confident before venturing into the real dungeons.

### iPad & Mac Improvements

- **Configurable keyboard support**: Customize your movement and attack key bindings directly in Settings
- **Improved Mac compatibility** and overall stability on Mac and iPad

### Bug Fixes & Stability
- Various bug fixes and stability improvements throughout the game

## Level Editor: What's New

- **File import** for `.etdm` level packs
- **Enhanced maze generator** that now makes use of all configured element quantities
- Bug fixes and performance optimizations

---

Thank you for playing! More adventures await in the maze. Development is still very much ongoing - stay tuned for the next updates!
