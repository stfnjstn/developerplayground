The May 2026 update <span style="background-color: #4CAF50; color: white; padding: 3px 8px; border-radius: 12px; font-size: 0.85em; margin-left: 5px;">R4.5 - May 2026</span> for "Escape The Dungeon Maze" and the Level Editor is now **officially released**!

This release adds a brand new wearable item type, a weight system that affects gameplay, a refreshed action bar, and a big step forward for Drag & Drop on iOS.

## Wearables & Refactored Item Editor

<div style="display: flex; gap: 10px; margin: 20px 0;">
  <img src="/assets/2026/05/IMG_7045.PNG" alt="Wearable item editor with live 3D preview" style="width: 100%;">
</div>

The Level Editor gains a new game item type: **Wearables**. These come with a dedicated editor and a fully refactored game item dialog featuring a **live 3D preview** — so you can see exactly what an item looks like before publishing your level.

The **Materials Editor** now lets you tint 3D models and adjust roughness, metalness, and opacity per material, giving level creators much more control over item appearance.

## Weight System & GameItem Info Screen

<div style="display: flex; gap: 10px; margin: 20px 0;">
  <img src="/assets/2026/05/IMG_7047.PNG" alt="GameItem info screen with detailed stats" style="width: 100%;">
</div>

Game items now have a **weight attribute**. Carrying too much will penalise your heroes' movement speed and food consumption — so managing your inventory becomes a real strategic consideration. A new **GameItem info screen** shows detailed stats so you always know what you're picking up.

## Improved Drag & Drop on iOS

Drag & Drop has been significantly extended on iOS — you can now drag items:

- From and to the **floor and alcoves**
- Between **chests and inventory**

Combined with the per-hero inventory introduced in R4.4, item management is now smoother than ever.
