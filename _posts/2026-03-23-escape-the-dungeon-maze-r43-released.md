The March 2026 update <span style="background-color: #4CAF50; color: white; padding: 3px 8px; border-radius: 12px; font-size: 0.85em; margin-left: 5px;">R4.3 - March 2026</span> for "The Dungeon - Get Out" and the corresponding Level Editor is now **officially released**!

This is a big one — Apple TV support is finally here, and game controllers now work across all platforms!

## Apple TV

<div style="display: flex; gap: 10px; margin: 20px 0;">
  <img src="/assets/2026/03/ETMTV1.jpg" alt="The Dungeon - Get Out on Apple TV" style="width: 100%;">
</div>

"The Dungeon - Get Out" is now available on **Apple TV**! Explore the dungeon on the big screen — crawl through corridors, battle monsters, and solve riddles from the comfort of your couch.

## Game Controller Support

<div style="display: flex; gap: 10px; margin: 20px 0;">
  <img src="/assets/2026/03/GameController.jpg" alt="Game Controller Support" style="width: 100%;">
</div>

Game controllers are now fully supported across **iOS, iPadOS, Mac, and Apple TV**. Connect your favourite controller and enjoy a more immersive dungeon crawling experience on every platform.

## Improved Apple Watch Controls

<div style="display: flex; gap: 10px; margin: 20px 0;">
  <img src="/assets/2026/03/ETMWatch1.png" alt="Apple Watch Improvements" style="width: 48%;">
</div>

The Apple Watch experience has also been improved with better and more responsive controls, making it easier to navigate the dungeon on your wrist.

## What's New

- **Apple TV port** — play on the big screen
- **Game Controller support** — iOS, iPadOS, Mac, and Apple TV
- **New door textures** — dungeons look better than ever
- **Improved Apple Watch controls**
- **Render fixes**:
  - Fountain
  - Alcove
  - Hero magic view
  - Monster kill animation positioning

## Bug Fixes & Stability
- Various bug fixes and stability improvements throughout the game
